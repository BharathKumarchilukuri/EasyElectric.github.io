For Your Information

  • Can we add multiple connections ?
    Yes. You can add multiple connections and manage them.
  • What do we need to register ?

    For registering into E-Bill,

    1. User need to have enter basic details like name, mobile number.
    2. you should have atleast one connection of your own, which is not registered on E-Bill.

  • How to Pay Bills ?

    For paying bills,

    1. First, You need to have an account on the E-Bill.
    2. Login to your account.
    3. Click on the Pay Bill on the top Navigation bar.
    4. Your connections will be displayed, choose a connection/s to pay the bill.
    5. Choose the mode of payment and make the payment.
